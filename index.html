Our Facilities

State-of-the-art infrastructure to support quality nursing education and training.

Nursing Foundation Lab

Provides hands-on training in basic nursing procedures with lifelike manikins and hospital-grade equipment.

A V AIDS Lab

Equipped with modern audio-visual tools to enhance teaching and student presentations using charts, models, and projectors.

Nutrition Lab

Designed for dietary planning and food preparation demonstrations related to patient care and therapeutic nutrition.

Pre Clinical Science Lab

Offers essential resources like models and specimens for learning anatomy, physiology, and microbiology.

OBC & Pediatrics

Specialized for maternal and child healthcare training with baby models, delivery kits, and pediatric tools.

Library

Houses an extensive collection of nursing literature, research journals, and digital resources for academic enrichment.

Hostel

Offers safe, hygienic, and well-maintained living spaces with facilities to ensure a comfortable stay for students.

Clinical Training
