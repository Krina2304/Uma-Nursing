Our Programs

Comprehensive nursing programs designed to meet the evolving needs of the healthcare industry.

B.Sc Nursing

4-year undergraduate program providing comprehensive nursing education with clinical rotations.

  • Duration: 4 Years
  • Eligibility: 10+2 with Science B Group with minimum 45%
  • Only For Female

GNM

3-year General Nursing and Midwifery program focusing on patient care and community health.

  • Duration: 3 Years
  • Eligibility: 10+2 Arts,Commerce,Science wuth minimum 40%
  • Only For Female

ANM

2-year program for GNM graduates to upgrade their qualification to B.Sc Nursing.

  • Duration: 2 Years
  • Eligibility: 12 Pass Arts,Commerce,Science
  • Only For Female

UMA Nursing College is committed to providing excellence in nursing education through innovative programs and clinical experiences that prepare students for successful careers in healthcare.
