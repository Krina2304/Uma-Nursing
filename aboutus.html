Our History

2023
College Foundation
UMA Nursing College was established with a vision to transform nursing education in the region, beginning with our first cohort of 70 students.
2023
First Accreditation
Received full accreditation from the National Nursing Education Board, validating our commitment to educational excellence.
2023
Expansion
Opened our new campus with state-of-the-art simulation labs and expanded our program offerings to include specializations in pediatric and geriatric nursing.

Our Facilities

Modern learning environments designed to prepare students for real-world nursing scenarios

Nursing Foundation Lab

Equipped with manikins and hospital setup to help students master essential nursing procedures in a real-world environment.

A V AIDS Lab

Features multimedia tools and visual aids for interactive teaching and student-led presentations.

Nutrition Lab

Designed for diet planning, food analysis, and hands-on learning in therapeutic nutrition and patient care diets.

Pre Clinical Science Lab

Provides essential models, charts, and specimens to support studies in anatomy, physiology, and microbiology.

OBC & Pediatrics

Dedicated space for maternal and child health practice, including delivery kits, infant models, and care simulations.

Health Sciences Library

Stocked with nursing textbooks, journals, and digital resources to support coursework and research.

Hostel

Secure, hygienic, and fully furnished accommodation promoting a healthy and focused campus life.
