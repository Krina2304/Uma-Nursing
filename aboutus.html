Our Mission

To educate and inspire future nurses through rigorous academic programs, hands-on clinical training, and compassionate mentorship, preparing them to excel in diverse healthcare environments.

Core Values

  • Compassionate patient-centered care
  • Evidence-based practice
  • Professional integrity
  • Lifelong learning

Our History

1995
College Foundation
UMA Nursing College was established with a vision to transform nursing education in the region, beginning with our first cohort of 42 students.
2003
First Accreditation
Received full accreditation from the National Nursing Education Board, validating our commitment to educational excellence.
2010
Expansion
Opened our new campus with state-of-the-art simulation labs and expanded our program offerings to include specializations in pediatric and geriatric nursing.
2020
Top Rankings
Recognized as one of the top 5 nursing colleges in the country for clinical training excellence and NCLEX pass rates.

Our Facilities

Modern learning environments designed to prepare students for real-world nursing scenarios

Simulation Labs

High-fidelity simulation environments replicating hospital wards, ICU, and emergency rooms for hands-on training.

Science Laboratories

Fully equipped labs for anatomy, physiology, microbiology, and biochemistry studies.

Comprehensive collection of nursing textbooks, journals, and online databases with dedicated study spaces.
